Iced Brown Sugar Latte

Description

The perfect bittersweet taste from the coffee and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz of espresso
  • 2 tsp of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cups of milk
  • ice

Steps

  1. Make 4 oz of espresso however you like, whether that's with instant espresso and some water or freshly ground coffee beans and a whole espresso machine.
  2. Pour the espresso into your glass.
  3. Mix in the brown sugar into the hot espresso.
  4. Pour as much ice to your liking in your glass.
  5. Stir until it's all one color.
  6. Sip and enjoy!
