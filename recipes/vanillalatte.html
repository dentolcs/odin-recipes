Iced Vanilla Latte

Description

The most basic but reliable iced coffee.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz of espresso
  • 4 tbsp of vanilla syrup
  • 1/2 cups of milk
  • ice

Steps

  1. Make 4 oz of espresso however you like, whether that's with instant espresso and some water or freshly ground coffee beans and a whole espresso machine.
  2. Pour 4 tbsp of vanilla syrup into your glass.
  3. Pour as much ice to your liking in your glass.
  4. Pour the espresso into glass.
  5. Stir until it's all one color.
  6. Sip and enjoy!
